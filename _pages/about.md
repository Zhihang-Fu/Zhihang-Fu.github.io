# 🔆 About Me &#124; 个人简介 

I am an algorithm researcher at the Feitian Lab of [Alibaba Cloud](https://www.alibabagroup.com/), dedicated to deploying Qwen post-training into real-world business scenarios and creating tangible value. I received my Ph.D. from [Zhejiang University](https://www.zju.edu.cn/english/) in 2019, advised by [Prof. Yaowu Chen](https://person.zju.edu.cn/0088219#0), and my B.Eng. from [Chu Kochen Honors College](http://ckc.zju.edu.cn/ckcen/), Zhejiang University.

我目前在阿里云飞天实验室工作，致力于通过Qwen后训练，在实际业务场景中落地应用、创造价值。我 2019 年博士毕业于[浙江大学](https://www.zju.edu.cn/)，本科毕业于浙江大学[竺可桢学院](http://ckc.zju.edu.cn/)。

# 🎓️ Research Interest &#124; 研究方向

My research focuses on **LLM algorithm**, building key components of the post-training pipeline to deeply optimize Planning & Tool-Use capabilities at both model and system levels, reducing hallucination and accurately completing user tasks.
I lead a team that has delivered domain-specific LLM solutions across verticals including international sports events and education.

当前从事大语言模型算法研究，致力于通过构建后训练过程中的关键环节：Data/Trajectory Curation、Training Strategy、Reasoning Scaffolding、Fine-grained Benchmarking，深度优化模型和链路的Planning & ToolUse能力，降低幻觉并精准完成用户任务。

我带领团队在奥运会国际赛事、国内教育等场景均有行业化落地。[米兰冬奥基于阿里千问打造奥运官方大模型](https://www.news.cn/tech/20260205/4f7888c48cb6439f9c13acd0058df517/c.html)，并在奥运会期间上线olympics.com官网，服务全球用户。
